The statins statins: drug interactions of significance to the dental practitioner .
This report outlines the potential for concomitant use of dentally prescribed medication to increase the risk of statin- statin-induced myopathy .
The associated signs and symptoms of statin- statin-induced myopathy are also highlighted , as well as the need for an accurate and updated drug history for all dental patients .
Clinical Relevance: This article describes the recognized adverse interactions associated with concomitant drug therapy for patients on statins .
