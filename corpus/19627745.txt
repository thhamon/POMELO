[Food intolerance] intolerance]. .
Adverse food reactions are common in the general population .
Nevertheless , our knowledge of the structure of food allergens and of the mechanisms involved is poor .
In 1995 the European Academy of Allergology and Clinical Immunology suggested a classification based on the causative pathogenic mechanism .
According to this classification , non-toxic reactions can be divided into food allergies when they recognize immunological mechanisms and food intolerance when there are no immunological implications .
The treatment of food intolerance is avoidance of the particular food .
There are specific treatments for some food intolerance ( beta-galactosidases for the management of lactose intolerance ) .
